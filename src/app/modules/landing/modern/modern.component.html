Frequently Asked Questions
Here are the most frequently asked questions you may check before getting started
What is the duration of the free trial?

Our app is free to try for 14 days. If you want more time, you can provide payment details which will extend your trial to 30 days, giving you an additional 16 days to try our app.

Are there discounts for non-profits or educational use?

Yes, our Personal and Premium packages are free for non-profits and educational use. Email your details to us after starting your free trial and we will upgrade your account if you qualify.

What is the storage used for?

Since we provide an extremely detailed reporting and analytics tool, it requires quite a bit of storage space. For average use, you don’t have to worry about running out of space since the Personal package limits the number of projects you can have.

If for some reason you run out of space, contact us and we will see what can be done about it and make sure you are not generating unnecessary reports or analytics data.

What happens if I’m not satisfied?

If you are still in your free trial period, you can cancel your account at any time with a single click. If you have already paid for your first month, we also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked.

After the first month, you can still cancel your account at any time, but we will calculate the amount that corresponds to the days you have used our app for that month and refund only the remaining amount.
